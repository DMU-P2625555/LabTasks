Dogs Become Valuable Employees at Bank

It was revealed this morning at a major high street bank has begun employing clever canines to perform basic data-entry tasks in several of it's UK data centres. In a statement earlier today a spokesperson for the bank said that the training had gone well with the first intake of ten pooches, and that overall they were just happy to be involved.

"I've made new friends, we have a great canteen, and in a few months I will have earned enough to put a deposit down on a new kennel. It's been a ruff few months but things are starting to look up."
